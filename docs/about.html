About the Team

Madelyn Smith

Madelyn Smith graduated from the State University of New York at Oswego with a BFA in Interaction Design and a minor in Communication in 2022. She is continuing her education at SUNY Oswego in the Human-Computer Interaction master's degree program. Currently, Madelyn is employed full-time as the Multimedia Designer for Campus Auxiliary Services at SUNY Geneseo. Madelyn enjoys crafting and spending time with her friends, family, and rescue dog named Zola.

Jaritza Jara

Jaritza Jara graduated from the State University of New York (SUNY) at Oswego with a bachelor's degree in Psychology and Sociology and a minor in Expressive Art Therapy in 2019. She stayed at SUNY Oswego and graduated with a Master of Science in Mental Health Counseling in 2021. She practiced therapy for 3 years and then enrolled again at SUNY Oswego in the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) program. Jaritza enjoys playing any and all video games, specifically Mario Party, and loves cats. She has a 4-year-old cat named Luna.

Zachary Smith

Zachary Smith graduated from Dutchess Community College with an Associate's degree in Liberal Arts & Sciences: Humanities & Social Sciences. He then moved on to the State University of New York (SUNY) at New Paltz, graduating with a degree in Computer Science and a Mathematics minor. He is currently enrolled in the Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) program at SUNY Oswego. In his spare time, he enjoys playing and creating games, reading graphic novels, riding his bike, and spending time with his dog, family, and friends. He is a dedicated student who dreams of working as an indie game developer.
